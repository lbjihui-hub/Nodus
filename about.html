About / get to know me

Systems-minded IT professional focused on infrastructure, identity, networking, and operational security.

I am a Memphis-based IT professional with eight years of hands-on experience across government, healthcare, and field engineering environments. I am not a generalist who dabbles. I have administered Active Directory for five thousand users, diagnosed network outages before they happened, intercepted live phishing attacks in real time, and presented original security research on wireless deauthentication methodology at Utah State University.

I completed a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management Information Systems at the University of Memphis in May 2026 while working full time in an enterprise government IT environment. I hold CompTIA CySA+, Security+, Network+, A+, and ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity.

This portfolio exists because certifications and titles do not fully communicate what someone can actually do. The writing, labs, and documentation here are evidence of how I think about systems, not just that I have studied them.

I am currently seeking roles in systems administration, security analysis, and network engineering where the work is technical, the environment is focused, and the expectation is that problems get solved rather than escalated indefinitely.
